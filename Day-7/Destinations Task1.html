DESTINATIONS PAGE CONTENT

Here are some amazing places you can explore through my travel guides:

Munnar (Kerala):
Famous for tea gardens, cool weather, waterfalls, and beautiful hills. Best time to visit: September to March.
Goa:
Known for beaches, nightlife, seafood, and water sports. Best time to visit: October to February.
Ooty (Tamil Nadu):
A peaceful hill station with lakes, gardens, and toy train rides.
