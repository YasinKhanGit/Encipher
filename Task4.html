Why Learn HTML?

HTML is the basic building block of all websites. Every webpage you see on the internet—from simple text pages to complex web applications—starts with HTML. It provides the structure for content, making it essential for anyone who wants to understand how the web works.

Learning HTML is easy and beginner-friendly, even if you have no previous coding experience. It helps you create headings, images, links, tables, and forms. Once you know HTML, you can quickly move on to CSS and JavaScript, which bring style and interactivity to your pages.

HTML skills open doors to web design, front-end development, blogging, and digital content creation. Whether you want to build your own website, start a career in tech, or improve your digital skills, HTML is the perfect place to begin. It acts as the foundation for all future web development learning.
